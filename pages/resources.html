The Benefits of Juggling

Here's an article from Wired about 8 reasons why juggling is great for you!

The New York Times investigated how juggling is a suprisingly good workout!

There's an article in Nature Neuroscience that showed juggling for 6 weeks produced beneficial changes in the white matter of the brain.

How to Juggle

Taylor Tries is a fantastic channel with information for beginners on how to start juggling, and tips and tricks for advanced jugglers. Check out her beginner tutorial here, and head to YouTube to see more.

The Jim Show is a pretty old fashioned looking website, but it will get you juggling three balls in no time!

The Benefits of Hiit

Here's an article from Heathline on 7 benefits of high-intensity interval training.

Vox produced a beginners guide to HIIT.

A journal article in the World Journal of Cardiology investigated how HIIT can improve your aerobic capacity and benefit your heart health.

Basic Exercises

Self have produced a gallery of 53 bodyweight exercises that can be done anywhere, many are included in JuggleHiit workouts.

If pictures and descriptions aren't your thing, head to youtube and there are many HIIT workout videos where you can see how the exercises should be done.
