The Balls

JuggleHiit balls come in five colours. As you practice juggling, some clever technology inside the balls measures how many catches you make and most importantly how many times you drop the balls. Every time you drop a ball, you do a high-intensity exercise matched to the colour of that ball.

×

The Exercises

When you drop a ball, you have to do a high-intensity interval associated with its colour. These exercises require no equipment, and are sure to get your blood pumping. Expect push ups, russian twists, squat thrusts and much more!

×

The App

The mobile app syncs the data from the JuggleHiit balls, and tells what exercises you need to do and for how long. You can choose the area of focus, the length and intensity of the workout. It also tracks your juggling progress and rewards you for your longest streaks without a drop.
